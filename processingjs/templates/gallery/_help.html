  • Sharing Artwork
    The Artwork you create is only visible to you, until you decide to Share it.
    To Share Artwork with friends or other students:
    1. View your Artwork
    2. Click SHARE link at the top
    3. Select an Exhibition to Share into
    4. Click the Share button
    5. Select the Share URL to Copy it, and Paste anywhere.

  • Editing Shared Artwork
    For assessment purposes, Artwork may not be changed once it is Shared.
    You may UNSHARE your Artwork, but this will invalidate your Share URL:
    1. View your Artwork
    2. Click UNSHARE.
    3. Click the Yes, unshare. button
    4. Edit and Save your Artwork
    5. Click SHARE to share again. This will generate a new Share URL.
    Alternately, you may CLONE your Artwork.
    1. View your Artwork
    2. Click CLONE.
    3. Edit and Save your cloned Artwork.
    4. Click SHARE to share the new Artwork.
